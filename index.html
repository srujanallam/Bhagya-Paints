About Us

Since 2015, Bhagya Paints has been at the forefront of the paint manufacturing industry, setting benchmarks in quality and innovation. Our expertise lies in crafting premium paints and coatings for commercial, industrial, and residential applications, meticulously formulated to provide enduring performance and vibrant finishes. In 2019, we proudly achieved ISO certification, underscoring our unwavering commitment to excellence, quality management, and sustainable practices. At Bhagya Paints, our mission is to deliver creative, eco-friendly solutions that breathe life, color, and lasting protection into every surface.
